Vox

A push-to-talk intercom for your team. Click someone, hold spacebar, talk.

Getting started

  1. Open app.getvox.net and sign in with email or Google.
  2. Pick a team to join — paste an invite code, or create your own from the Welcome screen.
  3. Allow microphone access when your browser asks. Without it, you can't talk.
First call? The other person needs to be online too — green dot next to their name. If they're not online, they can't hear you.

Making a call

  1. Click anyone in your team list. The call connects in about a second.
  2. Hold Space to talk. Release to mute. (You can also hold the on-screen PTT button.)
  3. Click End on the call banner when you're done.

Calls are intercom-style — when you click someone, you go straight through. No ringing, no answer button. If someone calls you, your mic stays muted until you hold spacebar.

Status modes

Set your status from Settings → Status:

  • AvailableYou can be called and you can call others.
  • BusyVisible to your team but signaling you're heads-down.
  • DNDDo not disturb — calls won't connect to you.

Settings

Display name

What shows up in your team's user list. Changes save to your profile and update for everyone instantly.

Audio devices

Pick a specific microphone or speaker. Useful if you have a headset and want it routed there instead of the laptop mic. (Speaker selection only works in Chrome and Edge.)

Team

Copy your team's invite code and share it with new members. They paste it on the Welcome screen to join. Admins can create new teams; members can leave.

Install as a desktop app

Vox runs as a Progressive Web App — you can install it like a native app:

  • Chrome / Edge: click the install icon in the URL bar (looks like a small monitor with a down-arrow), or open the menu and choose "Install Vox…"
  • Safari (iOS / iPadOS): tap the share button, then "Add to Home Screen."

Once installed, Vox lives in your /Applications folder (or home screen) and runs in its own window without browser chrome.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • Space — push to talk (only when you have someone selected)
  • Esc — close dialogs

Troubleshooting

Other users don't appear in my list

You're probably on different teams. Check that you're both on the same team — the active team name shows at the top of the user list. Use the team picker to switch.

Status bar shows "Disconnected"

Network blip — the app reconnects automatically. If it doesn't recover within ~30 seconds, refresh the tab.

I can hear them but they can't hear me

Your mic isn't selected or permission was denied. Check Settings → Audio → Input Device and make sure the right mic is picked. If the dropdown is empty, your browser may have blocked microphone access — re-grant it via the URL bar's lock icon.

Calls drop after a few seconds

Likely a firewall or VPN blocking WebRTC. Try without the VPN, or whitelist relay.getvox.net.

I closed my browser and now I can't log back in

Sign-in uses Supabase magic links and Google OAuth. Check your email for the magic link, or try again with a different browser to rule out a cookie issue.
